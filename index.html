About Me

Hello! My name is Atishay Jain, and I am a B-Tech 3rd year student with a passion for coding and web development. My journey began with Python, where I had the opportunity to build a recommendation system for movies and develop a registration and login system with OTP verification using Python libraries and a graphical user interface. These projects gave me valuable experience in software development and the chance to explore my interest in building functional applications.

Recently, I decided to expand my skill set and delve into the world of web development. I've built a portfolio website for myself from scratch, which has been an exciting and rewarding process. As a fast learner, I enjoy challenging myself and exploring new technologies to find creative solutions to problems.

I am a highly motivated individual who never gives up on a challenge. I am always willing to put in the time and effort to learn new skills and improve my abilities. I'm excited to take on new opportunities and contribute my skills to any team or project I'm a part of. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I look forward to connecting with you!
